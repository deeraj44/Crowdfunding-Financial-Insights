The Story

Why I Built This Dashboard

Understanding the dynamics of crowdfunding campaigns through data visualization.

The Inspiration

I’ve always been fascinated by the power of ideas, and how platforms like Kickstarter help bring them to life. But I wanted to know:
What actually makes a crowdfunding campaign successful?
With thousands of campaigns being launched every month, I saw an opportunity to use data science not just to analyze performance but to help creators and investors understand the patterns behind success and failure.
This led me to a public dataset of Kickstarter campaigns from Kaggle, a perfect real-world case for turning raw data into actionable insights.

The Challenge

The dataset was large and messy, over 300,000 rows with inconsistent types, redundant data, and no relational structure.
Raw data lacked normalization, everything was in one table.
Date and numeric fields were poorly formatted.
Needed to categorize funding goals into meaningful buckets.
Built a normalized schema using SQL with 3NF principles.
Find the SQL file in the Github repo

The Solution

Once the data was cleaned and transformed, I exported it into Excel and connected it to Power BI to bring the insights to life. The dashboard transforms complex crowdfunding data into intuitive visualizations, helping creators optimize their campaigns and backers identify promising projects.
