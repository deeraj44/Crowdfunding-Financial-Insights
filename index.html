The Story

Why I Built This Dashboard

Understanding the dynamics of crowdfunding campaigns through data visualization.

The Inspiration

I’ve always been fascinated by the power of ideas — and how platforms like Kickstarter help bring them to life. But I wanted to know: What actually makes a crowdfunding campaign successful? With thousands of campaigns being launched every month, I saw an opportunity to use data science not just to analyze performance but to help creators and investors understand the patterns behind success and failure. This led me to a public dataset of Kickstarter campaigns from Kaggle — a perfect real-world case for turning raw data into actionable insights.

The Challenge

Crowdfunding platforms provide raw data, but extracting meaningful insights requires sophisticated analysis tools that most creators don't have access to.

The Solution

This dashboard transforms complex crowdfunding data into intuitive visualizations, helping creators optimize their campaigns and backers identify promising projects.
